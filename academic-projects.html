BMFA Payload Challenge

Module: Model Aircraft Design & Construction

Designed and constructed a payload-carrying model aircraft for the British Model Flying Association (BMFA) competition, demonstrating advanced aerodynamics and engineering principles.

Project Highlights:

  • Aircraft design optimized for payload capacity and flight performance
  • Weight and balance calculations for safe flight operations
  • Hands-on construction using composite materials and electronics
  • Flight testing and performance validation
  • Competition participation with documented flight logbook

Note: Detailed flight logbook and technical documentation available upon request.

Hybrid-Hydrogen Powered Regional Aircraft

Module: Aerospace Vehicle Design | March 2025

Optimisation of a 50-seat hybrid-hydrogen powered regional aircraft designed for 1000 nautical miles range.

  • Twin-motor electric configuration with 6-blade carbon composite propellers
  • Complete performance analysis: take-off, climb, cruise, landing phases
  • Zero CO2 emissions aligned with ICAO 2050 net-zero goals
